Our Human Body

Today, we are going to take a closer look at the human body.The human body is made up of 4 systems:

circulatory system

digestive system

excretory system

respiratory system

circulatory system

This is the system of blood vessels and organs that transports blood to all the cells in the body. It is made up of the heart and the blood vessels, including the veins, arteries, and the capillaries.

digestive system

This is the system responsible for digestion. This includes the teeth, mouth, esophagus, stomach, small intestine, large intestine, and colon.

excretory system

This is the system which includes the kidneys and bladder. It eliminates waste.

respiratory system

This is the system of lungs and connecting tubes that transports oxygen to the red blood cells and gets rid of carbon dioxide.
